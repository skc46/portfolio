Research Interest

I'm primarily interested in discovering novel magnetic materials (in particular Heusler alloys) for spintronic application, especially for memory and storage devices. The material of interest is half-metal, a material with a possibility of exhibiting both metallic and insulating (or semiconducting) behavior in different spin channels. I'm an experimentalist who synthesizes samples and characterizes various properties. However, I also enjoy ground state properties of material particulary by performing first principle calcuations.
